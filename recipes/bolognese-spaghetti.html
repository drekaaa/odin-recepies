Bolognese Spaghetti

Thick and Meaty Bolognese Spaghetti!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Bay Leaves
  • Sugar
  • Red Wine
  • Worcestershire Sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Saute garling and onion
  3. Brown the Beef
  4. Add everything else, give it a good stir then simmer for 20 minutes minimum up to 3 hours. Slow cooking makes the beef incredibly tender and the sauce develops extra flavour.
